About the Student Study Performance Dataset

This dataset explores how students' performance in tests is influenced by various factors such as gender, ethnicity, parental level of education, lunch habits, and test preparation courses.

Dataset Overview:

  • Size: 1000 rows x 8 columns
  • Variables: Gender, Race/Ethnicity, Parental Level of Education, Lunch, Test Preparation Course, Math Score, Reading Score, Writing Score.

Key Features:

  • Demographic Factors: Explore the impact of gender, ethnicity, and parental level of education on students' test scores.
  • Student Support: Analyze the effects of lunch habits and test preparation courses on academic performance.
  • Subject Scores: Assess students' scores in math, reading, and writing subjects.

Dataset Columns:

  • Gender: Sex of the student (Male/Female).
  • Race/Ethnicity: Ethnicity of the student (Group A, B, C, D, E).
  • Parental Level of Education: Highest level of education attained by the student's parents (e.g., bachelor's degree, some college).
  • Lunch: Type of lunch received by the student before the test (Standard or Free/Reduced).
  • Test Preparation Course: Whether the student completed a test preparation course before the test (Complete or None).
  • Math Score: Score achieved by the student in the math subject.
  • Reading Score: Score achieved by the student in the reading subject.
  • Writing Score: Score achieved by the student in the writing subject.
